DAWN

The Depression Anxiety Wellness Network (DAWN) was originally designed with the intention of helping those who suffer from mental illness or struggle with maintaining mental health. DAWN offers resources to help users through breathing exercises, journaling, smiling, and student specific resources for those in the district where DAWN was developed.

SAM

Student Assisted Memory (SAM) was a web-developed project to aid the special needs students of Point Pleasant Borough High School with remembering important information. The project utilized a database with user logins where these students could view and practice remembering things like their address, birth date, phone numbers, etc.
